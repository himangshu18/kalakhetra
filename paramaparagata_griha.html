🏠 Nu-Baro

Traditional hill dwelling built using bamboo.

Nu-Baro

Nu-Baro is one of the traditional houses of the Tiwa community. It is constructed mainly with bamboo, timber and thatch, reflecting the indigenous architectural knowledge and cultural heritage of the hill Tiwas.

The design of the Nu-Baro provides protection from the weather while maintaining proper ventilation. The house also serves as an important social and cultural space for family gatherings and traditional ceremonies.

Nu-Baro - Traditional House

Documentary showcasing the traditional Nu-Baro house of the Tiwa community, highlighting its unique architectural style, cultural significance, and traditional construction methods.
