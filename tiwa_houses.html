About Deka Chang

Deka Chang is the traditional hill dwelling of the Tiwa community. It is built on elevated platforms to protect from humidity and animals. Bamboo, timber and thatch are used freely locally, showcasing their deep connection with nature and sustainable living.

About Traditional Houses

The houses of the Tiwa tribe reflect their rich cultural heritage, traditional knowledge and harmony with nature. These structures are not just homes but also spaces for community bonding, rituals and preserving traditions from generation to generation.
